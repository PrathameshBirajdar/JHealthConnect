Help & Support

❓ How to book an appointment?

Go to the Appointments section, click "Add Appointment", and fill in the details.

❓ How do I register as a new user?

On the login page, click "Register" and fill out the form.

❓ Who can add doctors or insurance companies?

Only Admin users can add doctors, insurance, or appointments.

❓ Need further help?

Contact support at support@jhealthconnect.com.
